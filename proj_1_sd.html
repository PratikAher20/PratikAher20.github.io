Indian Institue of Space Science and Technology(IIST) is equipped with its own satellite ground station which is controlled, operated and maintained by students. INSPIRESat-1 was the first student made satellite from IIST which was launched into orbit on 14th Feb 2022 on PSLV C-52 rocket. Tracking the health of the satellite, downlinking the science data and commanding it regularly were the operations accomplished at ground station.
  • Work Done
    • Operated the INSPIRESat-1 satellite using UHF and S-Band Ground Station Antennas.
    • Configured an open source Ground Support softwares to decode the telemetry packet and send commands to the INSPIRESat-1 satellite.
    • Designed a GUI to automatically configure the ground support software.
    • Carried out maintenance work for both UHF and S-BAND Antennas.
    • Improved and Optimized INSPIRESat Telemetry Decoder
    • Trained many students for operating the ground station.
