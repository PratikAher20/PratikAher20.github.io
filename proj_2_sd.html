POEM which stands for PSLV Orbital Experimental Module is a platform over the fourth stage of the PSLV rocket. It was developed by ISRO to make use of the space environment for carrying out various scientific experiments till the platform is in orbit. A payload developed by ISRO was launched on the first POEM platform and IIST's ground station along with other ISRO ground station received the data from it. The data was voice recording of the Indian Preamble, Indian National Anthem and text embedded noise which contains 15 lines of preamble.
  • Work Done
    • Tracked the POEM-1 platform and recorded the data.
    • Decoded the recorded data to obtained all the 15 lines of preamble.
    • Showcase the recordings to all the visitors at ground station which also includes Indian Diplomat and Minister of External Affairs of India, Dr. S. Jaishankar.
